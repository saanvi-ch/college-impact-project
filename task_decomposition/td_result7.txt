Planner (to chat_manager):

To solve this problem, we need to calculate the probability that the highest roll among the four dice is exactly 5, and then find the numerator and the denominator of the reduced fraction representing this probability. Here are the steps to follow:

1. **Calculate the Probability of Rolling a 5 or Less:**
   For a single 6-sided die, the probability of rolling a 5 or less is 5/6 since there are 5 outcomes (1, 2, 3, 4, or 5) out of 6 total possible outcomes.

2. **Calculate the Probability of Rolling No 6s:**
   When rolling four dice, to ensure that no die shows a 6 (and thus the highest roll could be a 5), each of the four dice must land on one of the 5 sides that has a number less than 6. The probability of rolling a 5 or less on all four dice is then (5/6)^4.

3. **Probability of at Least One 5:**
   To have the highest roll be a 5, at least one die must be a 5. We will subtract the probability that none of the dice shows a 5 from the probability calculated in step 2. First, calculate the probability that none of the dice show a 5, which is the probability of rolling a 1, 2, 3, or 4 on all four dice. This probability is (4/6)^4.

4. **Calculate the Desired Probability:**
   Now, subtract the probability of getting no 5s from the probability of getting no 6s:
   Probability(highest roll is 5) = (Probability of ≤ 5 on all dice) - (Probability of ≤ 4 on all dice)
                                  = (5/6)^4 - (4/6)^4

5. **Compute the Values:**
   - Compute (5/6)^4: (5^4)/(6^4) = 625/1296.
   - Compute (4/6)^4: (4^4)/(6^4) = 256/1296.

   The difference gives us the probability we want:
   Probability(highest roll is 5) = (625 - 256) / 1296
                                  = 369 / 1296.

6. **Simplify the Fraction:**
   We can now simplify the fraction 369/1296. Both the numerator and denominator are divisible by 3:
   369/3 = 123, 1296/3 = 432. This gives us 123/432.

   We can simplify further by dividing by 3 again:
   123/3 = 41, 432/3 = 144. This gives us the reduced fraction 41/144.

7. **Find a + b:**
   Now that we have the fraction in its simplest form, we find a and b to be 41 and 144, respectively.
   a + b = 41 + 144 = 185.

Therefore, the correct answer is 185.
